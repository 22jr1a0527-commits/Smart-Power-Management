🧠 What is N-BEATS?
Our AI model predicts demand by learning 2 things: Trend (is demand rising?) + Seasonality (what time of day?). More accurate than simple averages.
💡 Why Forecast Matters
Know TOMORROW's peak load TODAY. Use it to: Start generators before peak hits, Buy power at best time, Prevent blackouts & overloads.
⚠️ Understanding Errors
If forecast was 400 MW but actual was 420 MW = 20 MW error. Small errors = good model. Big swings = something unexpected happened!
🎯 Operator Action
See peak at 6 PM tomorrow? Schedule extra generators NOW. See low load at 3 AM? Good time for feeder maintenance.
